If you want any of these items, the following rules apply:

Send me an email: bird{at}lorikeet.nl and tell me a little bit about why you want this record and where it needs to ship to. If you live in the direct neighborhood, know who I am and where I live, than you can obviously come and get it without any of the shipment costs.
