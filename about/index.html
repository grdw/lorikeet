"A Lorikeet is a small to medium sized arboreal parrot. The majority have very brightly coloured plumage."

Lorikeet is a vinyl DJ project and a blog started by Gerard Westerhof (ex-Drunken Marias, ex-Kofferbaque). With this DJ project he will try to explore the wonderful, the obscure, the oriental, the odd; sounds to amaze.
