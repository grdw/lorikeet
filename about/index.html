"A Lorikeet is a small to medium sized arboreal parrot. The majority have very brightly coloured plumage."

Lorikeet is a vinyl DJ project and a blog started by Gerard Westerhof (ex-Drunken Marias, ex-Kofferbaque). With this DJ project he will try to explore the wonderful, the obscure, the oriental, the odd; groovy sounds to amaze.

Lorikeet is looking for the sounds from the far east and the warm south. Tropical and exotic atmospheres in a funky / jazzy jacket. Records like you've never heard before. Embark on the dance floor or listen and discover your inner palm tree. Beware: the music can cause mild fever and nostalgia for warm countries.
